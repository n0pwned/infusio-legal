# Privacy Policy for Infusio IV Drip Calculator

Effective date: 2026-06-09

Infusio IV Drip Calculator is provided by Kristie & Kristine.

This policy explains how the Android app **Infusio IV Drip Calculator** handles data. The app package name is `com.n0pwned.infusio`.

## What the app does

Infusio IV Drip Calculator is a formula-based IV calculation support app. It helps users calculate values such as drip rate, pump rate, dose infusion rate, and compatible rate conversions from values they enter.

The app is intended for calculation support only. It does not provide clinical decisions, medication safety checks, drug recommendations, treatment recommendations, patient records, or protocol guidance. Users remain responsible for verifying values, orders, units, institutional requirements, and any required independent checks.

## Data we collect

We do not collect personal data through the current version of the app.

The app does not include account login, cloud sync, analytics, advertising SDKs, billing SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, remote configuration, push notifications, or location services.

The app also does not request access to camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, phone, calendar, or location permissions.

## Calculation values entered in the app

When you enter calculation values, the app uses them locally on your device to produce formula-based results. These values may include volume, time, drop factor, rate, medication amount, diluent volume, ordered dose, or weight, depending on the calculator being used.

These values are not sent to us or to a server.

The current app does not intentionally save calculation inputs or results as history, templates, patient records, or cloud data. Values may remain visible on screen or temporarily in app memory while the app is open or while Android keeps the app process alive.

## Patient information

Please do not enter patient identifiers into the app.

Infusio IV Drip Calculator is not designed to collect or store patient names, medical record numbers, dates of birth, room numbers, diagnoses, clinical notes, medication order screenshots, or other patient-identifying information.

## Data sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share personal data with third parties.

We do not transmit calculation inputs, medication names, patient identifiers, or calculation results to third parties.

## Network access

Core calculator functionality works locally on the device.

The current release version of the app does not require Internet access for calculator use.

## Data retention and deletion

Because we do not intentionally collect or store personal data in the current app, there is no server-side user data for us to delete.

You can clear local app state through Android system settings by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

## Children

The app is not directed to children. It is intended for healthcare professionals, students, instructors, and other users who need formula-based IV calculation support.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Security

The app is designed to keep its core calculation workflow local to the device. The current app does not use accounts, cloud sync, analytics, ads, billing, or a server-side calculation workflow.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app's features, data practices, permissions, or store requirements change. When we make changes, we will update the effective date above.
